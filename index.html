Frequently Asked Questions

How long does professional teeth whitening take?

Our advanced whitening treatment typically takes 60-90 minutes. You'll see immediate results after just one session, with improvements of up to 8 shades lighter.

Is professional teeth whitening safe?

Yes, our professional whitening treatment is completely safe and FDA-approved. Our dental experts carefully monitor the entire process to ensure optimal results while protecting your teeth and gums.

How long do the results last?

Results typically last 6-12 months with proper care. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and touch-up recommendations to help maintain your bright smile for as long as possible.

Will I experience sensitivity?

Our advanced formula is designed to minimize sensitivity. While some patients may experience mild sensitivity, it's typically temporary and subsides within 24-48 hours.

What's included in the treatment cost?

Your treatment includes a consultation, professional whitening session, aftercare kit, and follow-up appointment. We also offer a satisfaction guarantee and flexible payment options.
